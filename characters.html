Luhzio

Our main character is Luhzio, the only non hybrid animal in the island. It is an aventurous purple tiger with pink strips, with a big heart. Luhzio always wanted to be like the other Bam Banimals, but now its flaw is the only thing that can save the other Bam Banimals.

Potatzio

Potatzio is Luhzio's best friend, together they live near the shore. Potatzio is a combination of rabbit and fox, making it easy for it to jump into high places, like the Lion's Cave in the mountain. Potatzio loves to shine, thats why its tail has some sparks that move when it moves.
